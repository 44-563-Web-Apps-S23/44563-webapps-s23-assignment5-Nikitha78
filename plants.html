Red star cordyline

Red star cordyline s a compact plant, with evergreen long, thin, arching, bronze-red leaves. It's ideal for growing in pots and is well suited to growing in sunny border alongside other tropical plants such as cannas, or on the patio.

Abelia

Abelia is an ornamental shrub that is under-appreciated and underused in many parts of the country and is worth a second look.Abelia ‘Kaleidoscope’ is one of the easiest, most versatile plants for your garden.

Abelia Image
Valerian

Valerian can grow to be just over 6 feet tall and has a strong odor. It seems to act like a sedative in the brain and nervous system. Valerian has been used as a traditional medicine dating back to ancient Greek and Roman times.
